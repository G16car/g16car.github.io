Axion Robotics

Axion Robotics is an innovative company founded by a student startup team, specializing in developing smart cars equipped with robotic arms. The company combines advanced robotics technology with intelligent control systems to create smart cars with precise operational capabilities. The product has wide applications in fields such as automation, logistics, and education, providing more efficient and flexible solutions for various industries.
