Mechanical design

From modeling to physical objects

Degree of freedom

By rotating parts up and down, rotating parts left and right, etc., a multi-degree of freedom system is built to realize complex motion trajectory planning in three-dimensional space, and it can flexibly reach various positions in the work space to meet diversified operation needs.

Motion accuracy

Servo-tower Pro MG995 Servo offers precise Angle control, enabling precise Angle adjustment to sub-degree levels. This enables the robot arm to control the error within a very small range when performing operations such as grasping and placing, and to meet the exacting task scenarios such as precision assembly and micro-operation.

Velocity of motion

Our robotic arm can complete the Angle switch in a short time, and combined with the lightweight structural design of the robot arm, it can achieve a higher motion speed and improve the working efficiency per unit time.
